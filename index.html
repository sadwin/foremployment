About us

Investland provides users with an easy opportunity to become an angel investor, by obtaining equity stakes in early-stage startups, which need funding to fuel their growth.

We carefully pick and analyze projects for risks and potential capital gains, with only the best of the best being placed on our platform. Based on this research, we offer a choice of 3 different investment profiles in terms of risk and return levels.
